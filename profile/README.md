## About Me

I'm a software developer focused on building reliable, well-architected applications and understanding the systems underneath them. I work across the stack — from user-facing apps to low-level system customization — with an emphasis on clean, maintainable code.

- **Application development** — I build and maintain [ZiMusic](https://github.com/Jigen-Ohtsusuki/zimusic), an Android music app integrating Spotify, JioSaavn, and YouTube Music. *(Rebranded and moved from the now-archived [ViMusic](https://github.com/Jigen-Ohtsusuki/ViMusic) repo.)*
- **Systems work** — I've contributed to [android_device_realme_X2](https://github.com/Jigen-Ohtsusuki/android_device_realme_X2), working with device trees, kernel tweaks, and performance optimizations.
- **Side projects** — I build things to sharpen fundamentals, such as a [ray tracer](https://github.com/Jigen-Ohtsusuki/ray-tracer) written out of interest in computer graphics and rendering.

I'm generally exploring the space between systems-level development and application design, and enjoy turning ideas into working software.
